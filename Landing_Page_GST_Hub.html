FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to register for GST to use this?+
No. The sheet works for both registered and unregistered freelancers. If your turnover is below ₹20 Lakhs, GST registration is optional — but this tool helps you track when you're approaching that threshold.
Does this work in Google Sheets?+
Yes! The file is delivered as .xlsx and works perfectly in both Google Sheets and Microsoft Excel. Simply upload to Google Drive and open with Google Sheets — all formulas and dropdowns work as-is.
What's the difference between GST and TDS?+
GST is collected BY you from your client and deposited to the government. TDS is deducted FROM your payment by the client on behalf of the government. This sheet tracks both — GST you owe, and TDS you can claim back via ITR.
Is this a replacement for a CA?+
For day-to-day tracking and estimation — yes, absolutely. For official filing (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, ITR), we always recommend consulting a CA. Think of this as your preparation tool that makes your CA's job (and bill) much smaller.
Will this work for future financial years?+
Yes. The sheet is reusable — just duplicate the Invoice Tracker tab for each new financial year. The formulas and structure remain the same. It's a one-time purchase, forever yours.
